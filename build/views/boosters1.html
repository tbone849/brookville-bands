Boosters

The Brookville Band Boosters is a parent organization that provides moral, physical, and financial support to the junior and senior high school band programs, its directors, and students. The Boosters meet the first Tuesday of each month from August-May at 6:00pm in the Band Room. An e-mail reminder goes out the week of the meeting as well as any cancellation notices.

We would love to have you as a member! If you are interested in being a part of the Brookville Band Boosters, please contact us and we can give you more details and information. Thank you!
